At ecopoints, our mission is to provide a fun and simple way to make your everyday life more sustainable!

Whether you're just starting your eco-friendly journey or you're already a sustainability pro, ecopoints helps you track your green actions and see the difference you're making.

Every time you log a sustainable action, whether it's biking to work, reducing food waste, or using a reusable cup, you earn Ecopoints! Your Dashboard keeps track of your progress, helping you build eco-friendly habits while seeing your progress over time.

How It Works

Every time you take a sustainable action—like recycling, using public transport, or reducing food waste—you log it in ecopoints. Each action earns you ecopoints, helping you build eco-friendly habits while seeing your progress over time.

What You’ll Find

Insights & Trends

A Personalized Dashboard

Motivation & Growth

Watch your Ecopoints grow as you develop a new greener lifestyle!

Why ecopoints?

We know that making sustainable choices can sometimes feel overwhelming. That’s why we’ve created a simple, rewarding system that turns everyday actions into meaningful change.

By tracking your actions, we hope you stay motivated and inspired to keep making a difference.

Start small, track your progress, and join a growing movement of people making the world a greener place—one Ecopoint at a time!
